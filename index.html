How to Play

Defend Earth from alien invaders! Move your ship and shoot down the aliens before they reach the bottom of the screen.

  • / or A/D: Move ship
  • Space: Fire
  • P: Pause game
  • F: Toggle fullscreen

Mobile Controls:

  • Touch and swipe to move
  • Touch to fire automatically
  • Tap exit button to leave fullscreen

Power-ups:

  • Rapid Fire: Shoot faster
  • Shield: Temporary invincibility
  • Multi-shot: Fire multiple projectiles
  • Bomb: Destroys all enemies in a radius
