Frequently Asked Questions

Is Space Invaders free to play?

Yes, Space Invaders is completely free to play in your web browser. There are no hidden fees or in-game purchases required to enjoy the full game experience.

Can I play Space Invaders on my mobile device?

Currently, Space Invaders is optimized for desktop and laptop computers. While it may work on some tablets, we recommend playing on a computer for the best experience. We're working on a mobile-optimized version that will be available in the future.

How do I report a bug in the game?

If you encounter any bugs or issues while playing Space Invaders, please use the contact form above and select "Report a Bug" from the subject dropdown. Please include as much detail as possible, including your browser type and version, and the specific steps to reproduce the issue.

Do you plan to add more games in the future?

Yes! We're currently working on recreating several other classic arcade games. Our roadmap includes games like Pac-Man, Galaga, and Asteroids. Stay tuned for updates on our website or follow us on social media for announcements.

Can I use Space Invaders for educational purposes?

Absolutely! We encourage the use of our games in educational settings. If you're a teacher or educator interested in using Space Invaders as a teaching tool, please contact us for additional resources and potential customizations for your classroom.
